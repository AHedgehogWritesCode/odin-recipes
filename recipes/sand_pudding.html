Mud pies

Description

A pudding that kids will love to make, and no one will really want to eat

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups sand
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1 Cup random colorful dirt or leaf material

Steps

  1. In seperate container add water to dirt or colorful leaf material
  2. Mix well per hand
  3. Add layers of sand to small plastic cups
  4. Layer colorful matter and sand
  5. Garnish with little umbrellas or something festive
  6. Serve and enjoy

    7. Note: serves four
